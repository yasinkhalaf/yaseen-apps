SaveLink Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 20, 2026

SaveLink is designed to help users save, organize, search, and open important links in one place. Your privacy is important to us.

Information We Collect

SaveLink does not require users to create an account and does not collect personal information.

The links, titles, notes, categories, and read status that you save in the app are stored locally on your device.

How Your Data Is Used

Your saved link data is used only to provide the app’s main features, such as saving links, organizing them into categories, searching, editing, deleting, and opening links inside the app.

Third-Party Websites

SaveLink allows you to open websites inside the app. Websites you open may have their own privacy policies, cookies, or tracking practices. SaveLink is not responsible for the content or privacy practices of third-party websites.

Advertising and Analytics

SaveLink uses Unity Ads to display advertisements inside the app. To deliver relevant and personalized ads, Unity Ads may collect and use device identifiers (such as the IP address, iOS Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), and app usage data) in accordance with its privacy policies.

If you grant permission via the App Tracking Transparency prompt, Unity Ads will use your IDFA to personalize ads. If you decline, you will still see ads, but they may be less relevant to you.

Data Deletion

You can delete saved links directly inside the app. You can also remove all locally stored app data by deleting the app from your device.
